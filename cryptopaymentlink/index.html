Learn it

What is Cryptopayment.link?

“Don’t buy it, earn it.” is the basic concept Nimiq aims to enable with cryptopayment.link. It allows everyone to charge crypto for services and products in seconds.

  • What is it about?

    It is about earning crypto instead of buying it. By accepting BTC, ETH or NIM for your effort/service, you get a piece of the future and widen your audience. The payment is purely peer-to-peer, that means it goes from the paying persons crypto wallet directly to yours, without a middleman and completely for free.

  • Who is it for?

    It is for all small business owners and freelancers who are open to accepting crypto payments. Think about diversifying your income directly. Instead of only getting paid in USD, EUR, etc, take a fraction of your income in crypto and hold it. A great alternative to opening an exchange account, transfering USD and trading into crypto.
