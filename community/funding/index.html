How does it work?

From individuals to corporates – anyone with an interesting Nimiq related idea and a plan on how to execute it, can apply for funding.

  • Step 1

    Check the Nimiq Forum Instructions and join the NimCubator to discuss your ideas.

  • Step 2

    Prepare your proposal following the template provided in the forum. Check this successful application to see how it’s done right.

  • Step 3

    Create your proposal in the proposal section of the Forum.

  • Step 4

    The community funding board meets regularly and will provide you an answer in the Forum and update the Nimiq Community through a post in the Nimiq Blog.
